## Instructions for Remaining Businesses

For the remaining 40+ businesses in the spreadsheet, follow this personalization pattern:

1. **Subject Line:** Replace [Business Name] with the actual business name
2. **Greeting:** Use owner's name if available, otherwise use "Hi there"
3. **Business-Specific Benefits:** Customize the bullet points based on business type:
   - **Restaurants/Cafes:** Menu sharing, online ordering, hours, special events
   - **Auto Repair:** Services, certifications, appointment booking, customer testimonials
   - **Retail Shops:** Product showcases, inventory, store hours, special offers
   - **Service Businesses:** Service descriptions, pricing, contact forms, testimonials
   - **Health/Beauty:** Services, before/after photos, appointment booking, staff credentials

4. **Location Reference:** Include the specific address when available to show local knowledge
5. **Industry-Specific Language:** Use terminology relevant to their business type
