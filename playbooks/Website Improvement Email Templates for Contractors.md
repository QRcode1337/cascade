## Email Template Instructions

### Personalization Guidelines:
1. **Research each business** before sending to add specific details about their services or location
2. **Customize the subject line** based on their primary service offering
3. **Include local market insights** when possible (weather patterns, local competition, etc.)
4. **Adjust ROI projections** based on their business size and market

### Follow-up Sequence:
1. **Initial email** (above templates)
2. **Follow-up #1** (3 days later): Share a case study of similar business success
3. **Follow-up #2** (1 week later): Offer free website audit or consultation
4. **Follow-up #3** (2 weeks later): Limited-time offer or seasonal opportunity

### Best Practices:
- Send emails Tuesday-Thursday between 10 AM - 2 PM for best open rates
- Keep emails under 200 words for mobile readability
- Always include a clear call-to-action
- Use local phone numbers when possible
- Follow up consistently but respectfully

### ROI Messaging Framework:
- **Immediate benefits**: More leads, better customer experience
- **Short-term gains**: Increased conversion rates, higher project values
- **Long-term value**: Market dominance, recurring revenue, referral growth

### Industry-Specific Pain Points to Address:
- **Seasonal businesses**: Year-round lead capture and customer retention
- **Emergency services**: 24/7 availability and rapid response capability
- **High-ticket services**: Professional credibility and trust building
- **Recurring services**: Automated scheduling and customer management
