## 🎨 Design & Layout Notes

### Color Scheme Suggestions:
- **Primary:** Deep blue or teal (trust, professionalism, tech)
- **Accent:** Bright orange or green (action, growth, energy)
- **Background:** White or light gray (clean, minimal)

### Typography:
- **Headline Font:** Bold sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter, Poppins)
- **Body Font:** Readable sans-serif (Open Sans, Roboto, Inter)

### Imagery:
- Hero section: Abstract tech/AI visual or professional headshot
- Process section: Icons for each step (audit, implementation, optimization)
- Social proof: Client logos or headshots (if available)

### Mobile Optimization:
- Large tap targets for CTA buttons (min 44×44px)
- Single-column layout on mobile
- Sticky CTA button at bottom of screen

### Page Structure (Carrd or Similar):
1. Hero (headline + CTA)
2. Problem (pain points)
3. Solution (what you offer)
4. How It Works (3-step process)
5. Services (what you build)
6. Pricing (transparent options)
7. Social Proof (testimonials + case study)
8. FAQ
9. About
10. Final CTA
