# Social Content Pipeline (Monthly Retainer)

Use this playbook to plan, draft, approve, schedule, and publish a monthly batch of social posts (target: **12–20 posts**).

## Monthly cadence

1. **Strategy alignment (Week 1):** confirm channel mix, campaign objectives, and offer priorities.
2. **Batch ideation:** create post concepts grouped by offer, objection, and proof themes.
3. **Draft production:** write caption draft, CTA, and assign creative asset link for each post.
4. **Client approval:** collect revision feedback and approval sign-off.
5. **Scheduling:** assign publish dates across the month.
6. **Publishing + reporting:** mark published and summarize performance for next month.

## Required metadata per post

- Channel (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, etc.)
- Objective (awareness, leads, sales, retention)
- CTA (book call, DM keyword, visit link, reply)
- Asset URL (drive folder, Canva link, final media link)
- Publish date
- Approval metadata (reviewer, approval status, feedback, approved timestamp)

## Suggested status flow

`DRAFT -> APPROVED -> SCHEDULED -> PUBLISHED`

## Quality checklist

- Hook in first sentence
- One clear CTA
- Brand-safe language and compliance check
- Correct tracking link or destination URL
- Visual asset attached and named consistently

## Retainer ops notes

- Keep all post records in one workspace-specific board.
- Aim for at least 1 week of scheduled buffer.
- Record blockers in approval notes to reduce next cycle revisions.
