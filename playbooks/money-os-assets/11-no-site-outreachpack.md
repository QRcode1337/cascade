🛠 The "No Website" Demo Checklist (Before you
send)
1. Clone your Generic Template: Use the Lead Qualification Chatbot from your Typebot
templates.
2. Personalize the Header: Change "AI Assistant" to "Welcome to [Business Name]"
.
3. Add 1 Specific Detail:
○
Restaurant: Upload a photo of their paper menu or type out 3 popular dishes.
○
Barber: Add their actual hours.
○
Contractor: Add a "Get a Quote" button.
4. Test the Link: Ensure it opens perfectly on mobile.
5. Send.
