## 🎯 Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips:
✅ Keep it under 50 characters
✅ Personalize with business name or pain point
✅ Avoid "salesy" language (no "Limited Time Offer!!!")
✅ Ask a question or create curiosity

### Body Copy Tips:
✅ Keep paragraphs short (2-3 lines max)
✅ Lead with value or observation, not your credentials
✅ Quantify everything (hours, dollars, percentages)
✅ Include ONE clear call to action
✅ Make it skimmable (bullets, bold, white space)

### Follow-up Cadence:
- Day 0: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Check-in if no response
- Day 7: Final soft close
- Day 30-60: Re-engagement with case study or update

### Conversion Benchmarks:
- Cold email open rate: 20-30%
- Reply rate: 5-10%
- Audit booking rate: 2-5% of sends
- Close rate (audit → deal): 30-50%

**To book 3-5 audits in Week 1, send 50-100 emails.**
